Pancakes

Breakfast

20 min · Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add the milk and egg, then mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a pan on medium heat.
  4. Pour a small amount of batter into the pan.
  5. Cook until bubbles appear, then flip.
  6. Cook the other side until golden.

Comment: Simple fluffy pancakes, perfect for breakfast with honey, fruit, or syrup.
